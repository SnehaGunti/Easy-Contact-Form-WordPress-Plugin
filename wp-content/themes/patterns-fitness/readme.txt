Patterns Fitness is a bold and dynamic block-based WordPress theme designed for gyms, personal trainers, fitness studios, and wellness coaches. It offers all the tools needed to build a strong and engaging online presence.

== Description ==

Patterns Fitness is a powerful and modern Full Site Editing (FSE) WordPress theme, ideal for gyms, fitness studios, personal trainers, and wellness coaches. Built with WordPress Full Site Editing, it gives you complete control to customize headers, footers, templates, and global styles directly from the Site Editor.

This theme features pre-designed patterns and layouts specifically created to highlight workout programs, trainer profiles, class schedules, pricing plans, success stories, and contact forms. Its fully responsive design ensures your website looks sharp and professional on all devices.

Whether you're promoting a fitness center, launching personal training services, or building a wellness brand, Patterns Fitness provides a robust platform to engage and grow your audience effectively.
